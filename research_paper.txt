
Factory Reallocation & Shipping Optimization System

Introduction
This project analyzes shipping performance for Nassau Candy Distributor and recommends better factory assignments.

Problem Statement
Shipping delays and inefficient factory assignments increase delivery time and logistics costs.

Methodology
Data analysis and predictive modeling were used to estimate shipping lead time and simulate factory reassignment.

Key Insights
Certain factories consistently produce faster shipping times.
Region and ship mode significantly affect delivery speed.

Recommendation
Use predictive analytics to assign products to optimal factories to reduce shipping lead times.

Conclusion
The system improves logistics efficiency and supports better business decisions.
